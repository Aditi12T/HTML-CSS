ABOUT

Salena Marie Gomez is an American Singer, Famous for so many songs and albums. She has also prove to a great actress as she has worked in some of the popular Disney shows like Wizards of Waverly Palace and also gave her voice to many hollywood movies.Time Magazine has named her one of the 100 most influential people in 2020. She worked with many charitable organizations and served as a UNICEF ambassador since age 17.

MUSIC STYLE

Gomez is described as a pop artist with a mezzo-soprano vocal range. Her work is primarily characterised as dance-pop and EDM.However, she has experimented with different music genres. Her debut album with the Scene was influenced by electronic rock and pop rock, while her subsequent records with the band opted for a dance-pop sound.
